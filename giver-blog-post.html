The Giver

The Giver is a story of a brilliant boy named Jonas who lives in a society that is being controlled by the rules and tradition of The Elders. Through his wisdom and mannerism, Jonas is selected as the Receiver of Memory, who is know as the giver. This post distinguishes him from others and gives him authority. He follows the rules and receives wisdom in the form of memories, but what he sees upsets him.

Review

The Giver was a riveting read that I'm certain I will reread on many occasions. So many moments in The Giver were so full of energy and life. While the Giver is a young adult book, it would definitely be enjoyed by all ages, with its beautiful descriptions. The simplicity and directness of Lois Lowry's writing gives the reader the chance to truly reflect on what they read. The giver is a believabe and timeless novel, the I'm sure will be appreciated for years to come.
